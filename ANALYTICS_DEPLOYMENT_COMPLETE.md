## 🎓 Data Displayed

### Automatically Calculated
```
Total Quizzes    → COUNT(*) from results
Average Score    → AVG(percentage) 
Best Score       → MAX(percentage)
Rank             → Position vs other students
High Score Rate  → % with 80%+ score
Consistency      → Days active * 10
Improvement      → (Latest - First) / First %
Weak Subject     → Lowest average
```
